# Game Instructions

## Objective
The objective of the game is to score more goals than your opponent within the stipulated time.

## Controls
### Player Movement
- **W**: Move Up
- **A**: Move Left
- **S**: Move Down
- **D**: Move Right

### Actions
- **Space**: Shoot the puck
- **Shift**: Speed Boost

### Special Moves
- **Q**: Perform a Special Skill Move
- **E**: Pass the puck to a teammate

## Game Modes
- **Single Player**: Play against AI opponents.
- **Multiplayer**: Compete against other players online.

## Scoring
- A goal is scored when the puck crosses the goal line.
- Rules regarding fouls and penalties apply.

## Tips
- Practice your movements and shooting skills in the training mode before jumping into a match!
- Communication with teammates enhances gameplay in multiplayer mode.
